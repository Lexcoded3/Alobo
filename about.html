About Joan

Meet Hon. Joan Acom Alobo

Hon. Joan Acom Alobo is the Woman Member of Parliament for Soroti City East, serving in the 12th Parliament of Uganda. A proud member of the Forum for Democratic Change (FDC), she brings passion, integrity, and a deep commitment to improving the lives of her people in the Teso sub-region.

Political Journey

From serving as Secretary General of the FDC Youth League to being elected to two consecutive terms in Parliament, Joan has consistently demonstrated leadership and dedication to democratic values.

Parliamentary Leadership

As Vice Chairperson of the Teso Parliamentary Group and a former member of the Committee on Science, Technology, and Innovation, Joan works tirelessly to bring development and opportunity to her constituency.
