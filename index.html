A Voice for Soroti City East in Parliament

Hon. Joan Acom Alobo is a dedicated leader committed to improving the lives of everyone in Soroti City East. As your Woman MP, she champions healthcare access, quality education, youth employment, and women’s economic empowerment across the Teso sub-region.

Grassroots Leadership

Joan engages directly with market vendors, farmers, and small business owners — listening to your concerns and bringing them to the national stage.

Proven Track Record

From serving as Vice Chairperson of the Teso Parliamentary Group to championing health and development initiatives, Joan delivers real results for her people.

Our Focus Areas

Six key priorities guiding our work to build a healthier, more prosperous Soroti City East.

Economic

Creating jobs and supporting small businesses to build a thriving local economy.

Development

Improving roads, water access and public infrastructure to connect communities.

Education

Championing quality schools, vocational training and bursaries for every child.

Social

Strengthening community welfare, social protection and support for the vulnerable.

Medical

Expanding healthcare access, maternal services and health facilities in every ward.

Business

Empowering entrepreneurs with capital, markets and skills to grow sustainable enterprises.
