Volunteer

You can help us to Bring the Change that we need

Building a prosperous Soroti City East takes all of us. Our ward action teams register community health concerns, mentor youth, and track local projects. I need volunteers who will tell me what is true, not what is comfortable.

We work in three shifts: weekday phone banks, weekend ward walks, and quarterly issue clinics. Tell us which fits you when you join.
